\subsection{Body, Font, and Line Styles}

The body style, font, and line style cannot be modified. The body is divided into two columns. The text is in Times font and size 10 points. The text must be justified on both margins. On the last page, the columns must be balanced. A new paragraph is indicated by indenting the first word.

\subsection{Figures and Tables}

Figures and tables may be placed within a column or within the body. In both cases, the figure or table is centered within the column or body. Both environments are numbered independently. The figure caption begins with \emph{Figure n:}, uses regular text at 10 points, and is placed below the figure. In contrast, the table caption is located above the table. Figure \ref{fig:fig1} shows a figure within the column, while Figure \ref{fig:fig2} is an example of a figure placed within the body.
